Our Story

Katherine and Lucas first met each other in Cambridge, Massachusetts on March 4th, 2011, albeit a year and a half before either would call that town their home away from home. Lucas was visiting Harvard as a prospective student and Katherine was visiting the Boston area to enjoy one of her last weekends as a student at the nearby Olin College of Engineering. They met through a mutual friend and had a great time together on that fateful day. Yet the time together was short-lived, as Lucas was headed back to Ithaca, New York the following day and would soon take a job in Los Angeles after the school year ended. Katherine would graduate from Olin College and continued on to graduate school in Boston. It seemed as though their time together was limited to that one day...

Until Lucas returned in late 2012 to Boston to start graduate school. Only a few hours back, Lucas contacted their mutual friend to inquire about Katherine. Reliving the day they first met, the story between them picked up where it left off nearly 2 years before, as if no time had passed between them. They reconnected immediately. And this time, the connection was permanent.

Through graduate school, traveling, and moving, the love between them only grew stronger over the years. In celebration of that love, every year they had their own Christmas together, exchanging gifts in their Cambridge home before going away to each of their family's homes. Lucas proposed to Katherine at their own Christmas in 2016 so that now they may call home where-ever they are together.
